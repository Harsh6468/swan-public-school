INFRASTRUCTURE

Far from the bustling urban environs, SPS Bihar Sharif is situated near the Army Cantonment at Danapur in village Chandmari. The sylvan and pristine surroundings lend a serene touch to the impressive campus spread over several acres. A marvel of modern architecture, the campus contains well-lit airy classrooms, outdoor theatre, performing art block, cafeteria, spacious courtyards for indoor games, and beautifully landscaped lawns. The sprawling playgrounds, the integrated sports complex, half Olympic-size swimming pool, gymnasium and the arena make the school distinct in its objective of incorporating sports as a basic component of the curriculum.

Apart from other basic facilities which are kept clean and hygienic at all times, there are computer labs, resource centers, cafeteria, and a fully equipped gymnasium. All the classrooms are networked with computers and connected to servers and CD caches for instant access to a world of information. Technology plays an important role in the lives of the students and is manifest in almost all activities carried out.

The growing need for maintaining ecological balance is keenly observed by the school and we strive to contribute to keeping the Earth green and clean. Thus, rain-water harvesting, recycling of waste and creating awareness about the need for green through plantations and botanical garden are just but a few initiatives carried out to uphold this objective. Segregating waste into organic and inorganic is another step towards the motto of RECYCLE – RECHARGE – REUSE and all students from classes Nursery to XII are imbibed with the much needed value of recognizing their contribution towards minimization of carbon footprint.

About SPS Patna


SPS Pune is one of the most trusted name in quality education, which is recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence .Swan Public School Pune was the second initiative of Takshila Educational Society. It was established in 2003 and is a collaborative effort with the Swan Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. SPS Pune is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII. In a very short span of time SPS Pune has established itself as one of the best K-12 institutions in western India. SPS has proliferated into a multidisciplinary institution, offering various facilities to impart varied expertise to keep pace with the world .The school offers courses that focus on holistic development of individuals by cultivating their unique talents.
